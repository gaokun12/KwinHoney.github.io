AUC-PR

December 10, 2021

Why AUC-PR

AUC-PR is a important indicator to test whether the classifier/learning model have a nice ability to make the correct predition. The prediction process usually happens a lot of area.

Definition of Precision and Recall

We usually test the values of precision and recall on testing datase [1].

Precision: $\frac{T_P}{T_P+F_N}$

Recall:$\frac{T_P}{T_P + F_P}$

The $n_1= T_P$, $n_2 = F_P$,$n_3 = T_N$, and $n_4 = F_N$.

The AUC-PR are the curve under pairs of values (Recal, Precision) under different thresholds. A example are shown in the figure:

Reference:

[1] Davis, J., & Goadrich, M. (2006, June). The relationship between Precision-Recall and ROC curves. In Proceedings of the 23rd international conference on Machine learning (pp. 233-240).
